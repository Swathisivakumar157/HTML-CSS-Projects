Dracaena Plants

Dracaena is a popular genus of plants that are commonly grown as houseplants. There are more than 170 species of dracaena, including some that are too big to be grown indoors. Regardless of their size, all dracaena plants have common spear- or grass-shaped leaves that grow from one or more thick, fleshy, cane-like main stems. Dracaena are appreciated for their foliage, which is often variegated. Some types of dracaena are tough to find while others are easier to purchase.
